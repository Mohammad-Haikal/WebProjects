Who Are We

A store that online sells different kinds of pet accessories and supplies including: food, treats, toys, collars, leashes, cat litter, cages, and aquariums. Our promise to pet owners is commitment, experience, and values built through years to build a better world for pets. Our Pet Advisors work at their best, to help you match your pet needs to their breed, condition, age, or life stage and get the answers you need.

GUIDE & HELP

Terms & Conditions

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Authorized employees within the store on a need-to-know basis only use any information collected from individual customers. We constantly review our systems and data to ensure the best possible service to our customers.

Shipping Method

We offer to ship to all cities in Jordan. the basic shipping costs are calculated depending on stated delivery rates. Most orders are processed and dispatched within (48 hours - 7 days) and our delivery service partners provide online parcel tracking facilities.

Payment Method

You can pay Cash on delivery, or online via your Debit or Credit Card.

Return and exchange

Exchange our products, we do not accept returns. In the event that you are not satisfied with your purchase, you must contact us within a day of receiving your order. We reserve the right to limit returns and exchanges, and can only offer one replacement per consumer.
